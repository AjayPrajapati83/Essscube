LOGO PLACEMENT INSTRUCTIONS
===========================

1. Save your ESSSqube Events logo as: public/logo.png
   - Recommended size: 200x200px
   - Format: PNG with transparent background
   - High resolution for retina displays

2. Update these files to use the actual logo:
   - components/layout/Header.tsx (line ~15)
   - components/home/HeroSection.tsx (line ~40)

3. Replace the placeholder div with:
   <Image src="/logo.png" alt="ESSSqube Events" width={48} height={48} />

FAVICON INSTRUCTIONS
====================

1. Replace app/favicon.ico with your actual favicon
   - Size: 32x32px or 64x64px
   - Format: .ico (preferred) or .png

2. Optional: Add apple-touch-icon.png (180x180px) to public/ folder
   for better iOS support
